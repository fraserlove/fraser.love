About Me

Hello! My name is Fraser and I am a 17 year-old Computer Science student from the UK who enjoys reading theoretical computer science, programming and general computer hardware and software interaction and discussion. I have recently finished my last year of High School and am going to study Computer Science and Mathematics at St Andrews University.

I have been programming for four years and throughout that time have created numerous programs in Python and C++. I find library programming most enjoyable because I love getting into the hard low level operations of computers and creating code which can be used and built upon by the community. I currently run Ubuntu, Arch and Windows 10 in a tri-boot setup on my computer and run with MacOS on my laptop.

I have been uploading to my YouTube channel for roughly four years and in that time I have created over 60 videos, about programming and technology in general. The channel currently has 500 subscribers and 200,000 views to which I try and upload to when I have the time. If you have any questions or want to contact me for another reason you can find a contact form at the bottom of the webpage.
